Something I Just Learned

So as I was browsing online, looking for a better way to host my website, I came across a site that talked about how to utilize Google Drive to host your website. WHAT!? I know! So basically how it works is

  1. You create a folder.
  2. Change the sharing to Public on the Web
  3. Looking at the url (https://drive.google.com/drive/u/1/folders/0B7L_2g6AwveidUM4TWJnQktFVGs) grab the part after the "folders" part.
  4. Essentially now, this folder acts as your root folder. If you go to www.googledrive.com/host/[your url part here], you will be up and running.
